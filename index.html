Our Story

Rooted in passion,
grown with care

Petal & Bloom was founded in 2014 by Eleanor Hayes, a trained horticulturalist with a vision for flowers that felt personal, not transactional. What started as a small studio in the East Quarter has grown into one of the city's most trusted floral ateliers.

We work closely with local growers, seasonal calendars, and our clients' own stories to design arrangements that genuinely resonate. Sustainability is woven into everything we do — from compostable wrapping to zero-waste studio practices.
